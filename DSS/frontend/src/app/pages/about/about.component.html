How it helps you

This decision support system is designed to assist students in choosing and refining thesis topics through a structured, data-driven approach. It combines a research-based questionnaire, keyword-matching logic, and transparent scoring.

The system evaluates essential dimensions like thesis type preference, working style, and interest areas. Each contributes to a personalized recommendation list, helping you understand why certain topics may be a stronger fit for your academic journey.

With an emphasis on accessibility and privacy, your responses are processed securely. The platform supports better communication with supervisors by providing actionable insights that guide your thesis selection process with confidence.

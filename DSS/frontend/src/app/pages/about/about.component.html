How it helps you

This decision support system offers structured guidance for students who face difficulty in choosing a thesis topic. Instead of relying on traditional search methods, it uses a rule-based, multi-step selection flow to collect each student's academic profile.

The system evaluates essential inputs including major, technical area, specific skill sets, and application direction. These selections are processed through the topic generation engine to synthesize thesis ideas that fit the student's background and goals.

By combining rule-based filtering, template selection, and recommendation scoring, the platform provides highly personalized thesis topics that students can review, save, and discuss with their academic supervisors.

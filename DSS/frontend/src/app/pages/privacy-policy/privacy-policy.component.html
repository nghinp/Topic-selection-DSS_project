Privacy Policy

Your data is yours. We are committed to protecting it and being transparent about how we use it to help you.

01 Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary to provide you with thesis topic recommendations. This includes:

  • Responses to the study field and interest questionnaires.
  • Keyword preferences for thesis topics.
  • Basic account information if you choose to register.

02 How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely to:

  • Generate personalized thesis topic recommendations.
  • Improve our scoring algorithms and recommendation logic.
  • Customize your user experience on the TopicInsights platform.

03 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All communication is encrypted via SSL/TLS, and sensitive data is stored securely. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

04 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and export your data at any time.
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-out of any non-essential data processing.

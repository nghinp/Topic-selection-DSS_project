Thesis Guides

Expert advice and step-by-step guides to help you navigate your thesis selection and research journey.

01 Finding Your Focus

The most critical step in your thesis journey is choosing the right field. Don't just pick what's popular—pick what sustainable for your interests and skills over several months.

  • Identify your core technical strengths.
  • Research current trends in your specific area of interest.
  • Evaluate the feasibility of the data or resources required.

02 Research vs. Application

Understanding the difference between a research-heavy thesis and an application-heavy project is crucial for your working style.

  • Research-based: Focuses on exploring new theories or improving existing methods.
  • Application-based: Focuses on building a practical solution or system using established technologies.

03 Working with Advisors

Your supervisor is your greatest resource. Come prepared with structured ideas and clear questions to make the most of your meetings.

  • Document your initial ideas before meeting.
  • Be open to constructive criticism and refining your topic.
  • Clearly define the scope of your project early on.
